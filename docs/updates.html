02.01.2021

White Paper Draft V2

Major changes made to the DAO

  • Instead of the BBBB team requesting for projects to be funded, holders can request and vote amongst themselves. This will allow all of our holders to be rock stars as more NFT projects would be reaching out to them instead of us.
  • If a project reaches a consensus, then the BBBB team will then perform DD and approve of the funds to be transferred. Results of our DD will be published in the members section.
  • For every 0.001 ETH requested, 1 vote is required. A request can be no less than 0.1 ETH and no more than 5 ETH. All requests expire after 30 days if it was not able to reach a consensus.
  • Only the holders that vote for a project will receive rewards from that project. This should encourage participation in the DAO.
